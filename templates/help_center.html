Frequently Asked Questions

To create a new event:

  1. Navigate to the Events section
  2. Click "Create New Event"
  3. Fill in the event details (name, date, time, location)
  4. Add event description and requirements
  5. Set pricing and capacity
  6. Upload event images
  7. Click "Create Event" to publish

To book a venue:

  1. Browse available venues in the Venues section
  2. Select your preferred venue
  3. Check availability for your desired date
  4. Choose your package and services
  5. Fill in booking details
  6. Complete payment to confirm booking

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Digital Wallets (PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay)
  • UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
  • Net Banking
  • Mobile Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe)

To cancel or modify a booking:

  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Navigate to "My Bookings"
  3. Find the booking you want to modify
  4. Click "Modify" or "Cancel"
  5. Follow the prompts to complete the action

Note: Cancellation policies vary by venue and event type. Check the specific terms before booking.
